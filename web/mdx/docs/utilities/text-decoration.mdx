## Usage

```html rose
<template preview>
  <div class="c-secondary-700">
    <p class="my-3 fz-sm td-none">
      Micheal Dwight Jim Pam
    </p>
    <p class="my-3 fz-sm td-underline">
      Micheal Dwight Jim Pam
    </p>
    <p class="my-3 fz-sm td-line-through">
      Micheal Dwight Jim Pam
    </p>
  </div>
</template>

<div>
  <p class="td-none">.  .  .</p>
  <p class="td-underline">.  .  .</p>
  <p class="td-line-through">.  .  .</p>
</div>
```
