## Usage

```html rose
<template preview>
  <div class="ta-center fz-sm mb-4">
    <p class="fw-600 op-70 c-pink-700">
      .ji-start
    </p>
    <p class="c-secondary-500 fw-500 px-5">
      Used to pushed toward the beginning of their axis.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="d-grid ji-start gtc-3 bd-brand bdw-3 p-2 c-secondary-700">
    <div class="m-2 fw-600 w-20 h-12 fx-center bg-purple-100 bd-purple-200">
      1
    </div>

    <div class="fxs-1 m-2 fw-600 w-20 h-12 fx-center bg-purple-100 bd-purple-200">
      2
    </div>

    <div class="m-2 fw-600 w-20 h-12 fx-center bg-purple-100 bd-purple-200">
      3
    </div>
  </div>
</template>

<div class="d-grid ji-start ...">
  <div class="...">1</div>
  <div class="...">2</div>
  <div class="...">3</div>
</div>
```
