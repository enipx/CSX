## Usage

```html rose
<template preview>
  <div class="c-secondary-700">
    <p class="my-3 ff-sans">
      Lorem Ipsum is simply dummy text of the printing and typesetting industry.
    </p>

    <p class="my-3 ff-serif">
      Lorem Ipsum is simply dummy text of the printing and typesetting industry.
    </p>

    <p class="my-3 ff-mono">
      Lorem Ipsum is simply dummy text of the printing and typesetting industry.
    </p>

    <p class="my-3 ff-brand">
      Lorem Ipsum is simply dummy text of the printing and typesetting industry.
    </p>
  </div>
</template>

<div>
  <p class="ff-sans">.  .  .</p>
  <p class="ff-serif">.  .  .</p>
  <p class="ff-mono">.  .  .</p>
  <p class="ff-brand">.  .  .</p>
</div>
```
