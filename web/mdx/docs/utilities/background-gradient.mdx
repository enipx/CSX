## Usage

```html rose
<template preview>
  <div class="my-3 bg-grad-sky c-white p-4 bdrs-md">
    It is a long established fact that a reader will be distracted by the readable content of a page when looking at its layout.
  </div>

  <div class="my-3 bg-grad-orange c-white p-4 bdrs-md">
    It is a long established fact that a reader will be distracted by the readable content of a page when looking at its layout.
  </div>

  <div class="my-3 bg-grad-vivid c-white p-4 bdrs-md">
    It is a long established fact that a reader will be distracted by the readable content of a page when looking at its layout.
  </div>
</template>

<div>
  <div class="bg-grad-sky">.  .  .</div>
  <div class="bg-grad-orange">.  .  .</div>
  <div class="bg-grad-vivid">.  .  .</div>
</div>
```

### Hover, Focus, Placeholder and Dark mode
