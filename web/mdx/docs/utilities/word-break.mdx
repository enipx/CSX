## Usage

```html rose
<template preview>
  <div class="c-secondary-700">
    <p class="mx-auto maw-56 my-5 bg-purple-100 wob-normal">
      Micheal Dwight Jim Pam Stanley Erin Creed Andy Robert Phillips Toby Kelly Carol Katrina Jane
    </p>

    <p class="mx-auto maw-56 my-5 bg-purple-100 wob-break-word">
      Micheal Dwight Jim Pam Stanley Erin Creed Andy Robert Phillips Toby Kelly Carol Katrina Jane
    </p>

    <p class="mx-auto maw-56 my-5 bg-purple-100 wob-break-all">
      Micheal Dwight Jim Pam Stanley Erin Creed Andy Robert Phillips Toby Kelly Carol Katrina Jane
    </p>
  </div>
</template>

<div>
  <p class="wob-normal">. . .</p>
  <p class="wob-break-word">. . .</p>
  <p class="wob-break-all">. . .</p>
</div>
```
