## Usage

```html rose
<template preview>
  <div class="c-secondary-700">
    <p class="mx-auto maw-56 bg-purple-100 whs-nowrap ov-hidden my-3 tov-ellipsis">
      Micheal Dwight Jim Pam Stanley Erin Creed 
    </p>

    <p class="mx-auto maw-56 bg-purple-100 whs-nowrap ov-hidden my-3 tov-truncate">
      Micheal Dwight Jim Pam Stanley Erin Creed 
    </p>

    <p class="mx-auto maw-56 bg-purple-100 whs-nowrap ov-hidden my-3 tov-clip">
      Micheal Dwight Jim Pam Stanley Erin Creed 
    </p>
  </div>
</template>

<div>
  <p class="tov-ellipsis">. . .</p>
  <p class="tov-truncate">. . .</p>
  <p class="tov-clip">. . .</p>
</div>
```
