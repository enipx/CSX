## Usage

```html rose
<template preview>
  <div class="c-secondary-700">
    <p class="my-3 tt-none">
      Micheal Dwight Jim Pam
    </p>
    <p class="my-3 tt-uppercase">
      Micheal Dwight Jim Pam
    </p>
    <p class="my-3 tt-lowercase">
      Micheal Dwight Jim Pam
    </p>
    <p class="my-3 tt-capitalize">
      Micheal Dwight Jim Pam
    </p>
  </div>
</template>

<div>
  <p class="tt-none">
    Micheal Dwight Jim Pam
  </p>
  <p class="tt-uppercase">
    Micheal Dwight Jim Pam
  </p>
  <p class="tt-lowercase">
    Micheal Dwight Jim Pam
  </p>
  <p class="tt-capitalize">
    Micheal Dwight Jim Pam
  </p>
</div>
```
