## Usage

```html rose
<template preview>
  <div class="my-3 bg-success-600 c-white p-4 bdrs-md">
    It is a long established fact that a reader will be distracted by the readable content of a page when looking at its layout.
  </div>

  <div class="my-3 bg-danger-600 c-white p-4 bdrs-md">
    It is a long established fact that a reader will be distracted by the readable content of a page when looking at its layout.
  </div>

  <div class="my-3 bg-warning-600 c-white p-4 bdrs-md">
    It is a long established fact that a reader will be distracted by the readable content of a page when looking at its layout.
  </div>
</template>

<div>
  <div class="bg-success-600">.  .  .</div>
  <div class="bg-danger-600">.  .  .</div>
  <div class="bg-warning-600">.  .  .</div>
</div>
```

### Hover, Focus, Placeholder and Dark mode
```html rose
<template preview>
  <div class="">
    <p class="my-3 my-3 bg-success-600 hover-bg-success-700 c-white p-4 bdrs-md">
      Hover to change background color 
    </p>
  </div>
</template>

<div>
  <p class="bg-success-600 hover-bg-success-700">
      .  .  .
  </p>
</div>
```
