## Usage

```html rose
<template preview>
  <div class="c-secondary-700">
    <p class="my-3 fw-400">
      Lorem Ipsum is simply dummy text of the printing and typesetting industry.
    </p>

    <p class="my-3 fw-500">
      Lorem Ipsum is simply dummy text of the printing and typesetting industry.
    </p>

    <p class="my-3 fw-700">
      Lorem Ipsum is simply dummy text of the printing and typesetting industry.
    </p>

    <p class="my-3 fw-500 hover-fw-700">
      Hover to bold text
    </p>
  </div>
</template>

<div>
  <p class="fw-400">.  .  .</p>
  <p class="fw-500">.  .  .</p>
  <p class="fw-700">.  .  .</p>
  <p class="fw-500 hover-fw-700">
    .  .  .
  </p>
</div>
```
