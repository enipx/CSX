  description="(similar to <code>Container</code>) sets the <code>max-width</code> property of an element to match the full width of the selected breakpoint when they reach their breakpoint. <br/> </br/>. By Default, The <code>Fluid</code> set an horizontal padding of <code>2rem</code> and <code>1.5rem</code> from <strong>md</strong> breakboint. This allows you to work faster without worrying about the space around your element content."
/>

## Usage

```html
<div class="fluid">
    .   .   .
</div>
```
