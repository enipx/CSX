## Usage

```html rose
<template preview>
  <div class="c-secondary-700">
    <p class="mx-auto maw-64 my-5 bg-purple-100 whs-normal">
      Micheal Dwight Jim Pam Stanley Erin Creed 
    </p>

    <p class="mx-auto maw-64 my-5 bg-purple-100 whs-nowrap ovy-auto">
      Micheal Dwight Jim Pam Stanley Erin Creed 
    </p>
  </div>
</template>

<div>
  <p class="whs-normal">. . .</p>
  <p class="whs-nowrap">. . .</p>
</div>
```
