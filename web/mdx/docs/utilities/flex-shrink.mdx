## Usage

```html rose
<template preview>
  <div class="ta-center fz-sm mb-4">
    <p class="fw-600 op-70 c-pink-700">
      .fxs-1
    </p>
    <p class="c-secondary-500 fw-500 px-5">
      Used to enable flex items to grow and fill any free space in its flex container.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="d-flex bd-brand bdw-3 p-2 c-secondary-700">
    <div class="m-2 ta-center fw-600 w-20 h-12 fx-center bg-purple-100 bd-purple-200" style="width: 60%">
      1
    </div>

    <div class="fxs-1 m-2 ta-center fw-600 w-20 h-12 fx-center bg-purple-100 bd-purple-200 bdw-3" style="width: 40%">
      2
    </div>

    <div class="m-2 ta-center fw-600 w-20 h-12 fx-center bg-purple-100 bd-purple-200" style="width: 60%">
      3
    </div>
  </div>
</template>

<div class="d-flex ...">
  <div class="...">1</div>
  <div class="fxs-1 ...">2</div>
  <div class="...">3</div>
</div>
```

```html rose
<template preview>
  <div class="ta-center fz-sm mb-4">
    <p class="fw-600 op-70 c-pink-700">
      .fxs-0
    </p>
    <p class="c-secondary-500 fw-500 px-5">
      Used to enable flex items to shrink.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="d-flex bd-brand bdw-3 p-2 c-secondary-700">
    <div class="fxs-1 m-2 ta-center fw-600 w-20 h-12 fx-center bg-purple-100 bd-purple-200" style="width: 60%">
      1
    </div>

    <div class="fxs-0 m-2 ta-center fw-600 w-20 h-12 fx-center bg-purple-100 bd-purple-200 bdw-3" style="width: 40%">
      2
    </div>

    <div class="fxs-1 m-2 ta-center fw-600 w-20 h-12 fx-center bg-purple-100 bd-purple-200" style="width: 60%">
      3
    </div>
  </div>
</template>

<div class="d-flex ...">
  <div class="fxs-1 ...">1</div>
  <div class="fxs-0 ...">2</div>
  <div class="fxs-1 ...">3</div>
</div>
```
